Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 20, 2026

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the beta — your name, email, and in-app usage. We never sell your data, and conversations are end-to-end encrypted.

1. Who we are

Chat&Go ("we", "us", "our") is the operator of the Chat&Go application and this website. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at chatngoapp@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

When you sign up for the beta we collect:

  • Account data: your full name and email address.
  • Preferences: native language, target language, platform choice, and stated goals.
  • Usage analytics: feature interactions, session length, and navigation paths — used to improve the product.
  • Crash reports: device type, OS version, and a stack trace when the app crashes — no personal content is included.

We do not collect payment information during the beta.

3. How we use your data

  • Send you a beta invite and product updates related to your sign-up.
  • Diagnose bugs and improve app stability.
  • Understand how features are used so we can improve them.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use your data for automated profiling, advertising, or sell it to any third party.

4. Conversations & messages

Chat messages inside the app are end-to-end encrypted. We cannot read the content of your conversations. Messages are never used to train machine-learning models. The only message-level data we access is what you explicitly flag to us for a support request.

5. Data sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Infrastructure providers (e.g. cloud hosting, email delivery) who are bound by data-processing agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal authorities if required by a valid legal request under applicable law.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion we will erase your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this does not affect prior processing).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at chatngoapp@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

This website uses only strictly necessary session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children

Chat&Go is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have done so, contact us and we will delete the data promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will notify beta users by email if we make material changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at chatngoapp@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
